Who is Chokecherry?

What is Chokecherry Hiding?

Chokecherry volunteered to be part of the military's soldier enhancement program. This program would augment a soldier's natural abilities through Drugs. Despite being assured that the procedure was fully researched and tested, it wasn't. The experiment skewed Chokecherry's worldview and now she sometimes fights for what is right and sometimes sees what is right differently.
